About

Born and raised in Manila, I was built-up with Filipino resiliency. An open person who is always curious, loves to keep on learning and trying new things. I'm looking for an entry-level job which involves programming and data. Looking forward to being exposed to a team environment, and working on my assertiveness and leadership skills.

I graduated from the University of Santo Tomas in Manila with a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology. The most valuable things I’ve learned and enjoyed in those years of formal education are programming fundamentals and learning programming languages like C, Java and Assembly. I also learned about information systems, SQL databases and Cisco networks.

I am also interested in creating videos, taking photographs on Instagram, as well as understanding psychology, esports games like Dota 2 and sandbox games like Rust and Cities Skylines.
